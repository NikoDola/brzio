# Digital Nectar: Mission & Vision

> Formerly known as **Logo Professionals**.

---

## Who We Are

Digital Nectar is a creative and technical ecosystem connecting clients with the best talent on the internet: graphic designers, UI/UX designers, software engineers, and IT specialists. Every professional in our network has **5+ years of real, in-the-field experience** working in professional companies, not just freelance side hustles.

We exist to make sure clients get the support they actually deserve, and that skilled workers get the recognition and opportunities they have earned.

But Digital Nectar is more than a talent platform.

## We Are Also an Education Company

Digital Nectar is also a learning hub. We teach:

- **Workers** how to sharpen their craft: branding, web development, app design, UI/UX, and the trends that genuinely matter in our industry.
- **Clients** how to understand the work they are paying for, in plain language, without the tech jargon that confuses people and lets bad actors hide behind buzzwords.

We believe a client who understands what they are buying becomes a better partner, and a worker who keeps learning becomes a better professional. So we bring both sides into the same education program, under one roof.

## Our Story

Digital Nectar started as **Logo Professionals**, an Instagram-based community focused on inspiring designers and sharing real-world design tutorials. From zero followers, we grew the page to **350,000 followers**, built entirely on the back of honest, useful content for designers.

That community made one thing clear: the world does not need another generic agency. It needs an upgrade. A brand that takes the same honesty, the same teaching spirit, and applies it to the full picture: design, development, education, and the freelance world itself.

Digital Nectar is that upgrade.

## Why "Digital Nectar"?

Think of nectar as the best of the best, gathered in one place, collected by working hummingbirds like Niko Dola and the team. A little bit of healthy workaholism from people who genuinely love what they do, distilled into a single place clients and creators can trust.

The name reflects what we offer: not a stream of average work, but the concentrated, refined, hard-earned best of an industry we have lived inside for **15+ years**.

## How We Work

Digital Nectar operates on a **project basis**. Clients come to us with a specific need, and we deliver a finished result. No retainers required up front, no vague ongoing commitments. You know what you are getting, you know what it costs, and we do the work.

This model matters because it removes the biggest friction in hiring creative and technical people: the fear of signing up for something you do not fully understand. A project has a beginning, a scope, and an end. That is something any client can evaluate honestly.

## Where We Are Starting

The first product Digital Nectar is selling is the **branding and web design calculator**: a guided configurator that walks a client through their logo design, logo redesign, or website project, gives them a clear price, and takes them straight to checkout.

This is not a side feature. It is the foundation of the business. Before we scale the talent marketplace, before we launch the full education program, we are proving one thing: that clients will trust us with a real project and a real payment. The calculator is how we do that.

It is built, it is live, and it is the first step in everything that comes next.

## What We Stand For

### Honesty Over Hype
We do not promote ourselves with empty marketing. We talk about what we have actually used, actually built, and actually learned. If we have no experience with a tool or a trend, we will not pretend we do.

### Education Over Mystification
We refuse to hide behind tech jargon. Our blog, tutorials, and client communication are designed to make complicated things understandable, because a confused client is an easy target for scammers, and we want fewer of both.

### Truth Over Politeness
We will publicly expose scammers, fake gurus, and exploitative platforms when we see them. We will also publicly celebrate the people doing honest, excellent work. Reputation should be earned, not bought.

### Experience Over Trend-Chasing
15+ years in the field is not a slogan. It is the filter we apply to every recommendation we make. We will write about AI tools we actually use, freelance platforms we actually work on, and competitions we have actually entered or judged.

## What We Will Cover
